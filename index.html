Welcome to my bakery

This is my website where you can purchase from a range of baked goods I produce. This is a one person buisness that I started, it began from a passion for baking and chocolatiering for my relatives which has now snowballed into a small buisness. Now after years of making and improving my own recipies I would like to share my creations with the world. All of my ingredients are fresh and locally sourced. Please take your time to browse my page and please contact if you'd like more details.

  4. Cupcakes

  6. Macaroons
